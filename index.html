ABOUT US

Welcome to Matlin Royale Academy, located at 4293 Rockview Park, Sunway City, Harare, Zimbabwe. We are a Christian primary school committed to fostering excellence in our students. At Matlin Royale Academy, we believe in nurturing not just the intellect but also the character of each child, preparing them to thrive in all aspects of life.

Our curriculum is designed to promote academic excellence, underpinned by strong Christian values. We offer a diverse range of sporting disciplines, encouraging our students to develop physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship alongside their academic pursuits.

Our team of highly educated tutors is dedicated to providing a supportive and engaging learning environment. They are passionate about their work and committed to helping each student achieve their full potential.

At Matlin Royale Academy, we strive to create a nurturing and inspiring atmosphere where children can grow into well-rounded individuals equipped for the future. Join us on this journey of excellence and discovery.

Activities

At Matlin Royale Academy, we believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals through a diverse range of activities that inspire creativity, critical thinking, and teamwork. From arts and crafts sessions that encourage self-expression to sports activities that promote physical health and camaraderie, our students have ample opportunities to explore their interests and develop new skills. With clubs and extracurricular programs covering everything from drama to coding, there's something for every child to discover and excel in. We foster a supportive environment where every student can thrive and grow, both inside and outside the classroom.
